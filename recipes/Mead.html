Honningbrew Mead

Description

Mead is an alcholic beverage made by fermenting honey, water and sometimes berries.

Equipment

  • 1 Gallon Jug
  • 1 Large pot

Ingredients

  • Brewsy Starter Kit
  • 2 Black Tea Bags
  • 2/3 Gallon Water
  • 3 1/2 Cup of honey

Steps

  1. Prepare your Tea

    In a large pot, heat 2/3 gallon (2.5 L) of water. Then, add 1-2 teabags to the pot. Stir, and let sit for 15 minutes.

  2. Add your honey.

    3. For dry mead, add 2 cups of honey (1.5 pounds).

    For semi-dry to semi-sweet mead, add 2.5 cups honey. (2 pounds).

    For sweet mead, add 3 cups of honey (2.5 pounds).

  3. Dissolve your honey in the tea, and let the mixture cool until room temperature.

    Pour into Gallon Jug when cooled.

  4. Add one full Brewsy bag

    5. Shake well for 30 seconds then put on the airlock

  5. Store in a warm, dark place

    6. Allow fermenting

  6. Wait 7 days, then taste-test
